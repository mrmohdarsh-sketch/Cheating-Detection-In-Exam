Abstract & Need for AI

Traditional proctoring methods are failing in the age of online education. Manual invigilation is limited by human fatigue and inability to monitor large groups simultaneously. Our project aims to bridge this gap using a **Multi-Modal AI System**.

The Core Problem:

  • Impersonation: Someone else taking the exam.
  • External Aids: Use of smartphones, books, or hidden notes.
  • Peer Communication: Verbal or non-verbal interaction during exams.

Our solution utilizes Real-time Computer Vision (CV) and Behavioral Analytics to flag suspicious activities with a high confidence score.
